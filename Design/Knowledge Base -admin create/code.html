Getting Started with Neeflow Automation

Automation is at the heart of the Neeflow ecosystem. By connecting your favorite tools and setting up intelligent triggers, you can eliminate manual tasks and focus on what matters most.

play_arrow

Preview: Automated Node Workflow

Setting up your first Trigger

Every automation begins with a trigger. This is the event that starts your workflow. In Neeflow, you can choose from over 500+ native triggers or use webhooks for custom integrations.

  • Navigate to the Automations tab in your sidebar.
  • Click on "Create New Flow" in the top right corner.
  • Select a trigger source (e.g., Shopify, Stripe, or Webhook).
  • Configure the specific event that should fire the flow.
"Automation is not about replacing human creativity, it's about giving humans more time to be creative."

Continue by adding actions to your workflow. Actions are the steps that Neeflow takes once a trigger is detected. You can chain as many actions as needed, including conditional logic and loops.
